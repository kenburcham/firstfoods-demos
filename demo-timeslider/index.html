2011

First Foods Policy

CTUIR FFP department collaborated with Oregon Department of Environmental Quality and the EPA to set new standards for fish consumption to better protect Indian health. Water Quality standards were adopted by Oregon and approved by the EPA that increased the daily fish consumption rate from 17.5g to 175g. This is very significant because fish consumption is used to calculate allowable polution in the water.
